Protomatic



  • With Protomatic you can check if a number is a prime number, find out the prime factorization of the number, and you can find the next prime.
  • For primality testing , prime factorization and finding out the next prime number program uses built-in PARI/GP functions: isprime , factor and nextprime.
  • You can use special characters to enter a number of special form.
